🎂 Birthday Reflection: Another Year Older, Still Counting Letters

Happy Birthday to me 🥳

Another lap around the sun. Another year to take life a little less serious, but somehow still overthinking the same things.

The world is loud. But sometimes peace is just sitting beside someone without even pressing your phone. Just being there.

Like that afternoon back in March. She was talking about work, one aunty was stressing her life, and I was there forming serious adviser. But truthfully, I was just watching her face. The way she says "see ehn" before she makes a strong point.

At some point she asked why I was smiling. I told her, "Nothing. I just like you."

She rolled her eyes, but that small shy smile still came out. You know that one they try to hide but you can still see it? Yeah, that one.

And somehow, that moment just stayed with me.

Then last week, she sent me a voice note at 2 AM. She couldn't sleep, was thinking about life, and just wanted to hear a familiar voice. I didn't sleep again that night. I just listened to her breathe between sentences. The way she sighs when she's tired. The way she says "goodnight" like it's a secret between us.

I realized something. Love isn't always grand gestures. Sometimes it's being the safe place someone returns to when the world is too loud. 💫

But anyway... 😅

Because it's my birthday, I decided to not stress myself too much… and instead lean into my small delusion.

So I brought back an old classic from childhood: 🔥 FLAMES 🔥

Yes, I know we are grown. Yes, I know situationships are supposed to be vague. But today, I wanted something that gives me answers, no matter how unserious it is.

The Contenders: 👤 Me · 👤 Her

The Process: I took both names. Crossed out matching letters like I was solving something important. Counted what remained. Ran it through the FLAMES sequence like my future depended on it. F. L. A. M. E. S. One by one… I removed them. Heart beating small.

The Result: 🥁 M. Marriage. 😂😂

Now, in my head, we've already picked colors. There is a small wedding somewhere in my imagination. In reality? My last message is still on "delivered" since Thursday. But the numbers have spoken. Who am I to argue with data?

So today, I'm just enjoying it. Enjoying the quiet moments. Enjoying the small smiles. Enjoying the fact that sometimes, life feels like something more than it actually is.

If you see me smiling to myself today, just know… FLAMES said I'm not single. Don't disturb my peace 😌

But guess what? I decided to share this delusion with you. Because why should I be the only one overthinking?

Cheers to another year. To life. To love. And to healthy delusions that make everything more interesting 🥂🚀
